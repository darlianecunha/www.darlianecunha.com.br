Summary

SUMMARY

I completed a postdoctoral fellowship at Erasmus University Rotterdam (2024–2025) and previously at the Autonomous University of Barcelona (2017–2018). I hold a PhD in Accounting and Finance from the University of Zaragoza (2016) and an MSc in Accounting from the University of São Paulo (2002). With over twenty years of academic experience, I have combined teaching, programme coordination, and research. Since 2018, my primary focus has been on research, consolidating a strong scholarly trajectory through publications in internationally recognised journals indexed in Web of Science and Scopus.

I have participated in numerous funded research projects in Brazil (CNPq, CAPES, FAPEMA), serving as principal investigator in five of them. I am currently engaged in a national project supporting COP 30 in Belém, the LabPortos research group, and the Onshore Power Supply (OPS) initiative at the Port of Itaqui. My research interests include the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), renewable energy, port decarbonisation, environmental disclosure and sustainable cities.
