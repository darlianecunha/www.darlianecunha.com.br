Summary

SUMMARY

Postdoctoral researcher at Erasmus University Rotterdam (2024–2025), with previous experience at the Autonomous University of Barcelona (2017–2018). I hold a PhD in Accounting and Finance (University of Zaragoza, 2016) and an MSc in Accounting (University of São Paulo, 2002). With over 24 years of academic experience, I have served as a lecturer and professor, combining teaching and course coordination with a strong research trajectory since 2019.

My work focuses on sustainability in the port sector, with publications in internationally recognised journals indexed in Web of Science and Scopus. I have secured competitive research funding in Brazil (CNPq, FAPEMA), led interdisciplinary projects, and actively contribute to the preparation of COP 30 through a national project aimed at developing sustainable port infrastructure in Belém.

My research integrates academic rigour with technological innovation, particularly through the use of Python and text mining for data analysis and sustainability reporting. Current research interests include the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), renewable energy, port decarbonisation, and environmental disclosure, reflecting a commitment to supporting global sustainability transitions.
